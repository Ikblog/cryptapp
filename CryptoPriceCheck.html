About the Crypto Price Checker

What is this app?

This app allows you to quickly check the current prices of various cryptocurrencies across different exchanges.

How to Use

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Enter the coin ID of the cryptocurrency you want to check (e.g., btc for Bitcoin, sol for Solana, trx for TRON).
2. Select the exchange you want to get the price from (Binance, Coinbase, or Bybit).
3. Click on the "Get Price" button to fetch the current prices.
4. To refresh the prices, click on the "Crypto Calculator" button.
5. To clear the results and input fields, click on the "Clear" button.
